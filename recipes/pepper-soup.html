Pepper Soup

Preparation Time:1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 500g assorted meats (such as goat meat, beef, or chicken), cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers (atarodo), chopped
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, peeled and minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Push onions to one side of the skillet and pour beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble eggs until cooked through.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and cooked meat (if using) to the skillet. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Stir in cooked rice and soy sauce. Mix well to combine all ingredients evenly.
  6. Continue cooking and stirring for 5-7 minutes until rice is heated through and slightly crispy.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot as a delicious side dish or main course.
