Pancakes

Preparation Time: Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Assorted meats (beef, goat meat, tripe)
  • 1/2 cup dried fish, cleaned and deboned
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring until a smooth batter forms. Ensure there are no lumps.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with vegetable oil.
  4. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake, spreading it slightly with the back of a spoon to form a circle.
  5. Cook the pancakes for about 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Remove the pancakes from the skillet and repeat with the remaining batter, adding more oil to the skillet as needed.
  7. Serve the Nigerian pancakes warm with toppings such as honey, syrup, jam, or fresh fruit.

Fun Fact:Nigerian pancakes are a popular breakfast dish enjoyed by people of all ages. They are similar to American pancakes but are thinner and slightly sweeter. Nigerian pancakes are often served as a quick and delicious breakfast option, and they can be customized with various toppings and fillings to suit individual preferences.
